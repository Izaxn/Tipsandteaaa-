Post-Service

Nail Aftercare
Guide

Protect your investment. Keep your nails lasting longer.

01

Hydrate Daily

Apply cuticle oil twice a day to keep the nail bed healthy and prevent lifting.

02

Protect Your Hands

Wear gloves when washing dishes or using harsh cleaning chemicals.

03

Don't Pick or Peel

If a nail starts to lift, do not pull it. Book a quick fix or removal appointment instead.

04

Nails Aren't Tools

Never use your nails to open soda cans, peel labels, or pry things open.

Before You Book

Salon Policies

💳 Non-Refundable Deposit

A $25 NON-REFUNDABLE deposit is required before your appointment is confirmed. This secures your slot.

⏰ Grace Period & Late Fee

5-minute grace period after your scheduled time. If I can still service you, a $15 late fee applies. After 5 min, appointment is cancelled.

🔄 Rescheduling

Notify me at least 48 hours in advance. Rescheduling requires another $25 deposit — both deposits are subtracted from your total.

🏥 Health & Safety

If there are cuts or open wounds on or around the cuticle, we will not be able to service you. Your safety comes first.

🖼️ Working Over Existing Sets

We do work over other people's work BUT a consultation is needed first — just a picture and video of the current set in question.

📅 Same Day Booking

Same day appointments may be available upon inquiry. 50% of total is due with same day bookings.

😊 Satisfaction Guarantee

If you are unhappy with the service received, please say so before leaving. We want you to walk out loving your nails.

🌊 Soak Offs

We do not advertise soak offs, however they are available upon request for an extra $10.

By booking an appointment, you acknowledge and agree to all policies listed above.
